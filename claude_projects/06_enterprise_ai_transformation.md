# Enterprise AI Transformation — Master Blueprint

## Series Vision

Establish Rahul Jindal as a top authority on Enterprise AI Transformation by covering what nobody else covers: function-specific AI transformation across Legal, Finance, IT, HR, Supply Chain, Operations, and Sales & Marketing.

**Differentiation:** Most AI content is tech-first or theoretical. This series comes from an enterprise insider who has seen AI transformation succeed and fail across every function. The angle is practical, function-specific, and grounded in real organizational dynamics — not vendor pitches.

**Tone:** Direct. Insider. Pattern-matching across functions. Never condescending, never hype-driven. Use specific stories (anonymized where needed) to ground abstract concepts.
