### Problem 2: The Organizational Metabolism Omnibus Got Only 55 Likes
This is your BEST intellectual work — a genuinely original framework with real depth. But it got fewer likes than a 3-line post about Uber drivers. **Why?**
- Long-form LinkedIn articles get crushed by the algorithm vs. feed posts
- No hook — it reads like a whitepaper, not a story
- No personal stakes — "why should I care right now?"
- No controversy or tension
- **Fix**: Break it into 10-15 punchy feed posts, each with a provocative hook. The Omnibus becomes the "book" that the posts drive traffic to.

- 2021-2022: High volume (many posts per month)

- 2025-2026: Very few posts, but higher quality (AI/Product focus)
- **LinkedIn algorithm punishes gaps.** Consistency > virality.
