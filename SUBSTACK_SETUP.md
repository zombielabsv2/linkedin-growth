**About Page:**

---

Every enterprise is having an AI conversation. Most of them are having the wrong one.

They're asking "what AI should we buy?" when they should be asking "how do we connect the seven conversations that determine whether AI actually transforms our business?"

I'm Rahul Jindal. I'm a Director at Google, where I've spent the last decade watching how large enterprises absorb — or fail to absorb — new technology. I've seen AI transformations succeed spectacularly and fail expensively, and the pattern is always the same: the technology works, but the organization can't digest what the technology produces.

**The Seven Conversations** is a weekly newsletter that goes function-by-function through enterprise AI transformation. Not the vendor pitch. Not the consulting framework. What actually happens when Legal, Finance, HR, IT, Supply Chain, Operations, and Sales each confront AI on their own terms.

Each issue covers one function or cross-cutting pattern:
- How AI is reshaping contract review, compliance, and legal governance
- Why the CFO's job shifted from producing answers to producing judgment
- Where supply chain AI delivers the fastest measurable ROI
- What HR learned about AI bias that every other function needs to know
- The pilot-to-production gap that kills 74% of enterprise AI initiatives

This isn't about what AI can do. It's about what organizations actually do with AI — and the seven conversations that make the difference.

Published weekly. Free.
