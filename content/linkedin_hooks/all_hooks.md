## Hook 4: IT — Hero and Bottleneck

Three shifts that fix it:
1. Federated AI teams (embed engineers in functions)

## Hook 8: The Seven Conversations

After watching dozens of enterprise AI transformations, I can now map why they succeed or fail.

It comes down to seven conversations:
1. Strategy (CEO) — "What's our AI thesis?"
2. Architecture (CTO) — "Platform or projects?"
3. Talent (CHRO) — "Who do we retrain?"
4. Risk (CLO) — "Who's accountable?"
5. Numbers (CFO) — "What's the real ROI?"
6. Operations (COO) — "Which processes change?"
7. Customer (CMO) — "How does our offer change?"

Most enterprises run 2-3 of these. Winners connect all 7.
